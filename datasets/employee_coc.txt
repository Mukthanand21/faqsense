Q: What is the purpose of the Employee Code of Conduct
A: The Employee Code of Conduct defines ethical standards professional behavior and responsibilities expected of all employees.

Q: Who does the Employee Code of Conduct apply to
A: The Code of Conduct applies to all employees interns trainees contractors consultants and third-party representatives.

Q: What behavior is expected from employees
A: Employees are expected to act with integrity honesty professionalism and respect at all times.

Q: What does professional conduct mean
A: Professional conduct includes respectful communication accountability and responsible behavior in the workplace.

Q: Are employees required to follow company policies
A: Employees must comply with all organizational policies procedures and guidelines.

Q: What is the organization’s stance on ethics
A: The organization promotes ethical behavior and zero tolerance for unethical practices.

Q: What is a conflict of interest
A: A conflict of interest occurs when personal interests interfere with organizational responsibilities.

Q: Are employees required to disclose conflicts of interest
A: Employees must promptly disclose any actual or potential conflicts of interest.

Q: What is the policy on confidentiality
A: Employees must protect confidential company and client information from unauthorized disclosure.

Q: Can confidential information be shared externally
A: Confidential information must not be shared externally without proper authorization.

Q: How should company information be handled
A: Company information must be used responsibly and only for legitimate business purposes.

Q: What is the policy on use of company assets
A: Company assets must be used responsibly and strictly for official purposes.

Q: Is misuse of company resources allowed
A: Misuse of company resources is strictly prohibited.

Q: What is the policy on workplace harassment
A: The organization enforces zero tolerance toward harassment or discrimination of any kind.

Q: What types of harassment are prohibited
A: Verbal physical sexual and psychological harassment are strictly prohibited.

Q: How can harassment be reported
A: Harassment complaints may be reported through HR or designated internal committees.

Q: Will harassment complaints be handled confidentially
A: Complaints are handled confidentially to the extent possible.

Q: What is the organization’s policy on discrimination
A: Discrimination based on gender caste religion race age or disability is prohibited.

Q: What is expected regarding workplace behavior
A: Employees must maintain a respectful inclusive and safe workplace environment.

Q: What is the policy on workplace violence
A: Any form of workplace violence or threatening behavior is strictly prohibited.

Q: What is the organization’s stance on alcohol and drugs
A: Use or possession of alcohol or illegal drugs during work hours is prohibited.

Q: What is the policy on health and safety
A: Employees must follow health and safety guidelines to ensure a safe workplace.

Q: Are employees responsible for reporting safety hazards
A: Employees must report unsafe conditions or hazards immediately.

Q: What is the policy on social media usage
A: Employees must use social media responsibly and avoid damaging the organization’s reputation.

Q: Can employees represent the company publicly
A: Only authorized employees may represent the organization publicly.

Q: What is the policy on gifts and hospitality
A: Employees must not accept gifts or favors that could influence business decisions.

Q: What is the policy on bribery and corruption
A: Bribery corruption and unethical inducements are strictly prohibited.

Q: What is the policy on compliance with laws
A: Employees must comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

Q: What is the policy on data privacy
A: Employees must protect personal and organizational data from misuse or unauthorized access.

Q: What is expected regarding IT and system usage
A: IT systems must be used securely responsibly and in accordance with company policies.

Q: What is the policy on email and communication
A: Official communication must be professional appropriate and compliant with company standards.

Q: What is expected during working hours
A: Employees must remain productive and focused on official duties during working hours.

Q: What is the policy on remote work conduct
A: Employees working remotely must maintain professionalism productivity and data security.

Q: What is the organization’s disciplinary policy
A: Violations of the Code may result in disciplinary action.

Q: What actions can lead to disciplinary measures
A: Misconduct policy violations or unethical behavior may lead to disciplinary action.

Q: What is the procedure for reporting violations
A: Violations may be reported through HR management or designated reporting channels.

Q: Are employees protected from retaliation
A: Employees reporting concerns in good faith are protected from retaliation.

Q: What is the whistleblower policy
A: The whistleblower policy allows reporting of unethical practices without fear.

Q: What is expected regarding cooperation during investigations
A: Employees must cooperate fully during internal investigations.

Q: What is the responsibility of managers under this Code
A: Managers must lead by example and enforce the Code of Conduct.

Q: Can the Code of Conduct be updated
A: The Code of Conduct may be revised periodically as required.

Q: Where can employees access the Code of Conduct
A: The Code of Conduct is available on the company portal or official repository.
