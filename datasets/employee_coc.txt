EMPLOYEE CODE OF CONDUCT

1. PURPOSE
The purpose of this Code of Conduct is to define expected ethical behavior and professional standards for all employees.

2. PROFESSIONAL BEHAVIOR
Employees must act with integrity, honesty, and respect toward colleagues, clients, and stakeholders.

3. CONFLICT OF INTEREST
Employees must avoid situations where personal interests conflict with organizational interests.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
All confidential company and client information must be protected and not disclosed without authorization.

5. USE OF COMPANY ASSETS
Company resources must be used responsibly and strictly for official purposes.

6. ANTI-HARASSMENT
The organization enforces zero tolerance toward harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior.

7. COMPLIANCE
Failure to comply with this Code may result in disciplinary or legal action.
