Q: What is corporate governance
A: Corporate governance refers to the system of rules practices and processes used to direct and control an organization.

Q: Why is corporate governance important
A: Corporate governance ensures accountability transparency ethical conduct and long term organizational sustainability.

Q: Who is responsible for corporate governance
A: The board of directors and senior management are responsible for corporate governance.

Q: What is the role of the board of directors
A: The board provides strategic direction oversight and ensures compliance with laws and policies.
