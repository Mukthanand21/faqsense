HUMAN RESOURCES POLICY MANUAL

1. PURPOSE
This Human Resources Policy Manual outlines the rules, procedures, and standards governing employment practices to ensure fairness, compliance, and consistency across the organization.

2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all employees, interns, contractors, and consultants employed by the organization.

3. EMPLOYMENT PRACTICES
All hiring, promotion, and termination decisions shall be based on merit, qualifications, and business requirements without discrimination.

4. ATTENDANCE AND WORKING HOURS
Employees are required to adhere to assigned working hours. Any absence must be communicated and approved through the prescribed process.

5. LEAVE MANAGEMENT
Employees are entitled to various types of leave including casual, sick, earned, and statutory leave, subject to eligibility and approval.

6. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
Employee performance will be evaluated periodically through structured appraisal processes aligned with organizational objectives.

7. COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS
Compensation and benefits shall be administered fairly and reviewed periodically in accordance with company policy.

8. DISCIPLINARY ACTION
Violation of HR policies may result in disciplinary action, including warnings, suspension, or termination.

9. POLICY REVIEW
This policy shall be reviewed periodically and updated as required.
